Sweet home-made pastry

There is no secret that all of us crave for some sugar especially when we are stressed! Seeing the tasty pastry in the bakery window makes it want even more! However, it would be better to bake it on our ownin case we want to save some money and enjoy the rusults of our cooking talent.

What do we need for baking?

  1. Go to the supermarket and buy puff pastry roll. The good point is: you do not have to make the dough by yourself oh, God, no, and save some time for yourself.
  2. Do not forget about the filling. You may buy some fresh apples, lemon and cinnamon.

Ingredients:

  • 1 puff pastry roll
  • 500 g apples
  • 1/2 tsp cinammon
  • 1/2 lemon
  • 50 g sugar

Let's bake it!

  1. Heat the oven up to 230 degrees C. We should place the pastry in the good preheated oven so to make our pies more fluffy.
  2. Slice some apples. Put them into the bowl, combine with cinammon and add some lemon juice in the filling.
  3. Add the apple mixture to the skillet and cook it over medium-high heat. Add some sugar into the mixture.
  4. Cut the dough into small square pieces.
  5. When the mixture is thickened, add the filling on a square piece of dough and make a triangle out of it so that the filling does not come out while baking in the oven.
  6. Place the pies on a baking sheet and bake until the crust is golden.
